Entertainment media

Not surprisingly, the most common forms of entertainment media that students in our CSCE 445 class enjoyed were

pc videogames such as Overwatch and League of Legends over console games

which probably has a lot to do with the major of our class.

for social media, mediums such as reddit and snapchat where you can be anonymous or much less transparent to others were much more popular than mediums such as Facebook or Twitter

which again is probably due to the demographics of people tending to be computer science majors.

A lot of people watch Twitch and popular shows such as the Office

As for the reason why people took this class, most took this class to learn how we communicate differently due to new media or to satisfy their degree plan.
